About Us

About Us Kanuri Translation and Communication Expert offers nationwide services translating from and into Kanuri language. we convert written materials from one language to another. our goal is for people to read the target language as is it were the source language. We offer Different translation services: Areas of expertise include translation of: Legal Documents, Patents, Trademarks & Copyrights, Medical Instrumentation & Biotechnology, Chemistry & Pharmacology, Educational, credentials, technical documents Translation of subtitles and books as well as Proofreading and specialized editing of an already translated text, In addition, we provide Kanuri interpreters for various legal and social events. Such as: Court Hearings & Trials, Sworn Statements & Investigations etc. Kanuri Literary Translation and Communication Expert provide a complete translation service. Our services are wider than converting a text from one language to another. In order to achieve the best result, at least four Kanuri language specialists would contribute in translating your text. Therefore, we can always provide top-end translation services regardless of the field or language of the source text. We respond as soon as you need us, and we provide a quality of work that would always satisfy your needs. Our translations always undergo editing and review. We are specialized in various subject fields. Such as: Economy and Banking, Law, Medicine, Advertising or Marketing. In order to maintain exceptional quality, we employ specialized expert-linguists with advanced degrees in scientific, medical and technical fields. Additionally, we guarantee the highest level of professional conduct and confidentiality. we use table top and fully-encapsulated and complete digital infra-red translation equipment sets to offer interpreter services for conferences, events seminar, meeting, education programmes etc..
