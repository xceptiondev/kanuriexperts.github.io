About Us

Kanuri Translation and Communication Expert offers nationwide services translating from and into the Kanuri language. We convert written materials from one language to another. Our goal is for people to read the target language as it is the source language. We offer Different translation services. Areas of expertise include translation of Legal Documents, Patents, Trademarks and copyrights, Medical Instrumentation and biotechnology, Chemistry and pharmacology, Educational, Credentials, Technical Documents, Translation of subtitles and books as well as Proofreading and specialized editing of an already translated text. In addition, we provide Kanuri interpreters for various legal and social events. Such as Court Hearings and trials, Sworn Statements and investigations, etc.
